Privacy Policy — Simple Screenshot

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Simple Screenshot is a Chrome extension designed to help users capture screenshots quickly and easily. Your privacy is important to us, and this extension was built with a privacy-first approach.

Data Collection

Simple Screenshot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension:

  • Does not track users
  • Does not collect browsing history
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not communicate with external servers

Screenshot Processing

All screenshots are:

  • Captured locally within your browser
  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Saved directly to your computer

No screenshots or image data are uploaded, shared, or stored externally.

Permissions

Simple Screenshot only requests the minimum permissions required to function properly, such as:

  • Access to the active browser tab for capturing screenshots
  • Download permission to save images locally

These permissions are used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension.

Third-Party Services

Simple Screenshot does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external libraries that collect user data.

Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure or misuse by this extension.

Changes to This Policy

If the extension’s functionality changes in a way that affects user privacy, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
